Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

🔒 Zenith does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Overview

Zenith ("we", "our", or "the app") is a focus and screen time management application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data from users. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect usage data or analytics
  • We do not collect device information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not use tracking technologies or cookies

Data Storage

All data generated by Zenith (your app blocking preferences, routines, focus sessions, and statistics) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

No Account Required

Zenith does not require you to create an account. You can use all features of the app without providing any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Zenith does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Children's Privacy

Zenith does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, this app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
